Neon Drift Runner Endless
Drift along the neon lane, collect Orbs to boost your multiplier, and avoid Hazards. Dash through tight gaps — but it has a cooldown.
Goal
Survive as long as possible. Score rises with speed + multiplier.
Dash
Tap Space (or Dash button) to become invincible briefly.
FX
Shake & particles
Controls
Drag / Tilt-like steer
Mobile ready
Tip: Stay near the center to react faster. Chain orbs to keep your multiplier high.
How to Play
Steer: ←→ or AD, or drag/swipe anywhere on the game.
Dash: Space (or Dash button). Short invincibility + burst speed. Cooldown shown as a ring.
Orbs: Increase multiplier and give a tiny heal to your multiplier decay.
Hazards: Crash ends the run unless you are dashing.
Score = distance × multiplier. Speed increases over time.
